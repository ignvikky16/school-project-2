Hi!

The International School of Hyderabad (ISH) is a non-profit, English medium international school located in Hyderabad, capital city of Telangana, India. The school serves students from overseas and Indian students who plan to do their post-primary education overseas. The school is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC) and the Council of International Schools (CIS). The Elementary School accepts students between the ages of 3 and 11 years of age. The Secondary School includes Grades 6 – 12, and is divided between the Middle School and the High School.
